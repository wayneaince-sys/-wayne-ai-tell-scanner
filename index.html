Wayne-mode audit

Cut the filler. Break the rhythm. Put some blood back in the sentence.

This pass scores cadence, repetition, hedging, abstraction, passive drift, and soft openers. It also runs distilGPT-2 in your browser to measure how predictable the prose is. For voice-level rewrites — the kind where "the damage" becomes "the doorway you stop in without knowing why" — it builds a Claude-ready brief you can paste straight into your real writing tool.
