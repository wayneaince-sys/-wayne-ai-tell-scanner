Wayne-mode audit

Cut the filler. Break the rhythm. Put some blood back in the sentence.

This pass scores cadence, repetition, hedging, abstraction, passive drift, and soft openers. It also runs a small distilGPT-2 model in your browser to measure how predictable the text is — the lower the perplexity, the more it reads like average LLM output. Nothing is sent to a server.
